Approaches to psychology:

Behavioural:
Classical conditioning:
Learning as a result of association.
Pavlov
Salivating dogs and bells
Unconditioned response, Neutral stimulus, Conditioned stimulus, Conditioned respose

Operant conditioning:
Learning as a result of reinforcment.
Skinner - skiner box
Positive reinforcement, negative reinforcement, punishment
Application - token systems in special need schools/prison

Pros:
- Scietific and simple - it has ideas that can be tested
- Real world application

Cons:
- Deterministic
- Could be seen as oversimplifying the human mind

Attention, retention, skill, motivation

from the experimenter depending on whether they see them get punished or rewarded.
