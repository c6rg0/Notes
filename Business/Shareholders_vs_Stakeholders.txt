A stakeholder is anyone who has an interest in the business, or who 
may be affected by the activities of the business.

Internal stakeholders are those inside a business who may be 
affected by corporate decision making;
1. Employees,
2. Managers,
3. Owners.

External stakeholders are those outside the organization who are
affected by decisions made by the business. These vary depending on
the business and its location, but the main general ones are;
1. Customers
2. Competitors
3. Suppliers
4. Local community
5. Pressure groups
6. Unions
7. Government
8. Shareholders

5)
Pressure groups are organizations that try to influence the 
government and other authorities to change policies or adopt 
specific goals, without seeking political power themselves.
They use methods like lobbying, public demonstrations, media 
campaigns and boycotting to raise awareness and pressure decision 
makers.


A shareholder is a person, business or organization that owns at
least one share of a company.
A sharholder is a stakeholder of the business.
